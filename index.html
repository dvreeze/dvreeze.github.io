Yaidom.

Yaidom is yet another Scala DOM-like library. It has precise querying and precise namespace handling in its genes. If you agree that these traits are more important for an XML library than offering an XPath-like experience, please keep reading.

Yaidom is described in a series of articles. The first one describes basic querying in yaidom. More articles will follow soon.
